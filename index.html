UX/UI Front End Developer

Contributing to create a better internet

Hello! I’m Andrea, UX/UI designer, front-end developer, and marketer based in Valencia.

I believe in the power of good design and communication to contribute making the internet a more accessible, creative and source of information environment.

I haven’t had the typical route to become a developer. I have a multi-faceted background in marketing and sales, but when I first touched a backend, I knew that was made for me.

I started out self-taught, teaching myself to code and design thorough web resources and doing digital projects for my company, friends and family.

Finally, I decided to enroll in the UX/UI and front-end development program at ESAT (Video Games Digital Media Development School) to become a full-stack front-end developer and UX/UI designer.

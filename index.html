Hello! My name is Andrea and I am a UX/UI designer, front-end developer, and marketer based in Valencia.

I believe in the power of good design and communication to make the internet a more accessible, creative, and source of information environment.

I would describe my design style as minimalist, clean, and balanced. I aim for compositions that are aesthetically pleasing and easy to understand for all audiences.

I took an unconventional path to become a developer. While my background is in marketing and sales, I have worked for small companies where I had to take on a variety of tasks. This is where I discovered my passion for web design.

Through self-study and training at ESAT (Videogames Digital Media Development School), I am working towards becoming a full front-end developer and pursuing my passion.
